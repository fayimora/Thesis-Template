\section*{Regular Expressions}
\label{sec:appx-regular-expressions}
The regular expression used to find links in our text is:
\begin{verbatim}
  {(https?:\/\/)?([\da-z\.-]+)\.([a-z\.]{2,6})([\/\w \.-]*)*\/?}
\end{verbatim}

The regular expression for finding twitter user names is:
\begin{verbatim}
  @[a-zA-Z0-9]+ ?
\end{verbatim}
